Executive Summary

  • An ETH-staking-and-stablecoin colossus, not a yield-strategy market. At $25M+, the census holds $68.2B — ~30× Base. The biggest block is restaking / liquid staking ($30.8B, 18 vaults), led by Lido stETH alone ($15.6B). ETH-denominated vaults are 56% of TVL; stablecoins 36%.
  • Extreme venue concentration. The top 5 protocols hold 59% of TVL and the top 10 hold 78%: Lido, Aave v3, Sky/sUSDS, Binance wBETH, SparkLend, Maple, ether.fi, Rocket Pool, Ethena, Spark.
  • Lending is the deepest vault category ($19.5B across 69 vaults): Aave v3 + SparkLend, curated Morpho vaults (Steakhouse, Gauntlet, Sentora, Sky.money), Fluid fTokens, and Maple institutional credit. Stablecoin-lending median net APY ≈ 3.89%.
  • Yields cluster low at scale; the high tail is small and flag-heavy. Blue-chip stable yields sit at 3–6% (sUSDS 3.64%, Aave/Spark USDC ~4%, Fluid fUSDC 6.1%). The 10–22% headlines (Convex cvxCRV, Saturn sUSDat, Mainstreet msUSD) are sub-$220M, incentive- or credit-driven, and multi-flagged.
  • "Markets" and "LP pools" ≠ "vaults." 65 of 80 exclusions are Morpho Blue isolated markets, Compound collateral legs, and Curve/Uniswap LP pools with no distinct deposit-and-earn vault token. Curated MetaMorpho vaults are included; the bare markets they allocate into are not.

depeg_risk (121). Stablecoins (fiat-peg), liquid-staking/restaking ETH derivatives (stETH/weETH/rETH/cbETH/osETH/ezETH), wrapped/pegged BTC (LBTC/eBTC/SolvBTC/wBTC), and synthetic dollars (USDe/USD0++/msUSD/sUSDat). Native ETH/WETH and alts (CRV, LINK, AAVE) not flagged.
2
centralized_dependency (101). Any active curator/manager/keeper/operator/off-chain process: every curated Morpho vault, every LST/LRT, all aggregator/basis-trade/leveraged-loop/RWA products, and governance-rate savings (sUSDS, sDAI). Permissionless money markets (Aave, SparkLend, Compound, Fluid, Curve LlamaLend) not flagged.
3
liquidation_risk (72). All lending & leveraged-loop vaults — suppliers face indirect exposure to borrower liquidations / bad-debt socialization; leveraged-loop vaults can themselves be liquidated.
4
withdraw_queue (28) + lockup (19) → 41 vaults can't exit instantly. Every LST (validator-exit / unstaking queue), Ethena sUSDe (7-day cooldown), Aave Umbrella stakes (cooldown), and KYC-gated RWA funds (BUIDL, USTB, OUSG, USDY, JAAA/JTRSY, USYC, thBILL, VBILL). Even "instant" lending vaults are subject to utilization.
5
il_risk (3). The Convex Curve-LP auto-compounders (FRAX-USDe, DOLA-sUSDe, ETH-stETH). No other vault in the set wraps a volatile AMM LP at $25M+.
6
smart_contract_unaudited (1). TermMax XAUE — no public third-party audit firm identified. Absence of the flag ≠ risk-free; all vaults carry generic smart-contract risk.
7
Concentration is the dominant systemic exposure. $15.6B (23% of the census) is a single contract — Lido stETH. Top 5 contracts hold 59%, top 10 hold 78%. A failure or depeg in Lido, Sky/USDS, or the major Aave/Morpho markets would propagate across most of the report.
8
Data integrity. APYs never fabricated — missing components are n/a; aggregate TVL is gross (heavy vault-in-vault nesting: Spark→Sky, curated Morpho→Morpho Blue, LRTs→stETH-class). 141/141 addresses returned bytecode; 137/141 matched symbol(). 2 candidate pools (~$50M) omitted as address-unverifiable.

Methodology

5-phase workflow · 38-agent fan-out

Phase 1 — Discovery

Pulled yields.llama.fi/pools, filtered chain=Ethereum & tvlUsd ≥ $25M → 223 pools (saved to raw_ethereum_llama.json) from 5,117 Ethereum pools. Per-pool net_apy_7d from yields.llama.fi/chart/{pool}. Snapshot 2026-06-04.

Phase 2 — Enrichment & classification (multi-agent)

223 pools grouped into 19 batches (Morpho 47, Aave 30, Curve 16, Spark 11, Compound 10…; tail chunked). One agent per batch resolved each pool's canonical Ethereum vault address (Morpho blue-api, bgd-labs Aave address-book, Compound roots.json, Fluid/Sky/Spark docs, Etherscan, protocol apps), audits, and withdrawal terms; a second agent adversarially re-verified every classification — catching fake/wrong addresses, AMM-LP/market/duplicate mis-includes, and missing risk flags.

Phase 3 — Categorization

Tagged each vault with category · underlying_asset_class · risk_flags · protocol_tier from the fixed taxonomies, derived from category, asset class, audit coverage, and withdrawal terms. Protocol tiers: Tier 1 = 98 · Tier 2 = 28 · Tier 3 = 15.

Perfil de riesgo (risk_tier)

Cada vault recibe un risk_score aditivo (mayor = más riesgo) y un risk_tier: Bajo ≤ 2 · Medio 3–5 · Alto ≥ 6. Es una heurística transparente de riesgo (independiente del rendimiento), no asesoría. Componentes del score:

  • Risk flags ponderados: smart_contract_unaudited +3 · il_risk +2 · lockup +2 · liquidation_risk +1 · centralized_dependency +1 · withdraw_queue +1 · depeg_risk +1
  • Tamaño (TVL): <$1M +2 · $1–10M +1 · $10–100M 0 · ≥$100M −1
  • Clase de activo: stable 0 · eth/btc/lp_token +1 · alt +2
  • Apalancamiento (leveraged_loop) +1 · dirección confianza media +1

Distribución: 66 Bajo ($33.73B) · 74 Medio ($34.42B) · 1 Alto ($26M).

Phase 4 — Report

Top 10 ranked by net_apy_30d − (risk_flag_count × 1%). CSV carries the 15 required columns plus underlying_asset_class, risk_score, risk_tier, protocol_url, protocol_tier (20 total).

Phase 5 — Validation

All 141 rows schema-complete (0 nulls in required columns); all addresses valid 0x/unique; every category, asset-class & risk-flag within its allowed set; APYs within 0–200%; TVL ≥ $25M. Every address re-verified on an Ethereum public RPC (ethereum-rpc.publicnode.com) for bytecode (141/141) + symbol() (137/141).

Exclusions (from 223 pools → 141 vaults)

  • 27 raw AMM/DEX-LP pools — Curve (16), Uniswap v3 (6), Uniswap v2, PancakeSwap, Balancer v3, Fluid DEX
  • 22 individual Morpho Blue markets — isolated markets with no distinct curated vault token (curated MetaMorpho vaults ARE included)
  • 16 Compound v3 collateral-only positions · 4 Pendle PT tokens
  • 4 duplicates (deduped by address) · 2 address-unverifiable (~$50M)
