Methodology

5-phase workflow

Phase 1 — Discovery

Pulled yields.llama.fi/pools, filtered chain=Arbitrum & tvlUsd ≥ $2M → 96 pools. Excluding DEX/perp/Pendle (42) → 54 candidates. Deduplicated by vault_address → 25 unique contracts.

Phase 2 — Enrichment

Protocols with >3 vaults on Arbitrum (Aave 9, Fluid 5) enriched from native sources / address registries — Morpho GraphQL, Beefy API, Yearn yDaemon. Sommelier API checked (0 Base vaults in set). Others used protocol docs/registries (bgd-labs aave-address-book, Compound roots, Fluid deployments, Moonwell/YO docs).

Phase 3 — Categorization

Tagged each vault with category · underlying_asset_class · risk_flags from the fixed taxonomies, derived from category, asset class, DeFiLlama ilRisk, audit coverage, and withdrawal terms.

Perfil de riesgo (risk_tier)

Cada vault recibe un risk_score aditivo (mayor = más riesgo) y un risk_tier: Bajo ≤ 2 · Medio 3–5 · Alto ≥ 6. Es una heurística transparente de riesgo (independiente del rendimiento), no asesoría. Componentes del score:

  • Risk flags ponderados: smart_contract_unaudited +3 · il_risk +2 · lockup +2 · liquidation_risk +1 · centralized_dependency +1 · withdraw_queue +1 · depeg_risk +1
  • Tamaño (TVL): <$1M +2 · $1–10M +1 · $10–100M 0 · ≥$100M −1
  • Clase de activo: stable 0 · eth/btc/lp_token +1 · alt +2
  • Apalancamiento (leveraged_loop) +1 · dirección confianza media +1

Distribución: 7 Bajo ($686.9M) · 16 Medio ($225.0M) · 2 Alto ($325.3M). Bajo concentra el 82% del TVL (blue-chips de stablecoins).

Phase 4 — Report

Top 10 ranked by net_apy_30d − (risk_flag_count × 1%). CSV carries the 15 required columns plus underlying_asset_class.

Phase 5 — Validation

All 95 rows schema-complete (0 nulls in required columns); all addresses valid 0x/unique; every category, asset-class & risk-flag within its allowed set; APYs within 0–200%; TVL ≥ floor; every address re-verified on-chain for bytecode + symbol() match.

Exclusions (from 382 pools)

  • 205 raw AMM/DEX-LP & perp pools (not vaults)
  • 8 individual Morpho Blue markets — shared singleton, no distinct vault address
  • 6 Compound v3 collateral-only · 6 Pendle PT/LP · 5 Fluid Smart Vaults (NFT positions) · 3 Dolomite Balance (singleton)
  • 1 sky-lending duplicate (same as Spark sUSDS)
  • 6 vaults with un-verifiable addresses (Concrete, Silo v2, Cian, Liminal, Zerobase)
