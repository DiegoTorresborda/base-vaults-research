Executive Summary

  • A stablecoin-lending monolith. lending is 57 of 96 vaults and 78% of TVL; stablecoins back 47 vaults / $1.64B (76%). Morpho MetaMorpho ($1.25B), Aave v3 ($354M) and Spark sUSDS ($222M) are ~85% of the market.
  • Yields cluster low on safe assets. Blue-chip USDC ~4.3–4.5%, Fluid fUSDC ~5.5%, Spark 3.65%. ETH vaults median ~1.8%, BTC ~0.1% (BTC is mostly collateral).
  • The high-APY tail is small and flag-heavy. Headline APYs reach 50–84% but on sub-$3M, alt/LP vaults that mostly gate withdrawals.
  • Withdrawal friction is widespread. 21 of 96 vaults carry withdraw_queue or lockup.
  • "Pools" ≠ "vaults." DeFiLlama's largest Base entry — a $2.37B cbBTC Morpho Blue market — has no distinct vault address and is excluded; that single item is most of the gap from the candidate universe.

Methodology

5-phase workflow

Phase 1 — Discovery

Pulled yields.llama.fi/pools, filtered chain=Base & tvlUsd ≥ $500K → 382 pools (saved to raw_llama.json). Deduplicated by vault_address → 95 unique contracts.

Phase 2 — Enrichment

Protocols with >3 vaults (Morpho 27, Moonwell 13, Aave 9, Beefy 6, Fluid 5, YO 4) enriched from native sources — Morpho GraphQL, Beefy API, Yearn yDaemon. Sommelier API checked (0 Base vaults in set). Others used protocol docs/registries (bgd-labs aave-address-book, Compound roots, Fluid deployments, Moonwell/YO docs).

Phase 3 — Categorization

Tagged each vault with category · underlying_asset_class · risk_flags from the fixed taxonomies, derived from category, asset class, DeFiLlama ilRisk, audit coverage, and withdrawal terms.

Perfil de riesgo (risk_tier)

Cada vault recibe un risk_score aditivo (mayor = más riesgo) y un risk_tier: Bajo ≤ 2 · Medio 3–5 · Alto ≥ 6. Es una heurística transparente de riesgo (independiente del rendimiento), no asesoría. Componentes del score:

  • Risk flags ponderados: smart_contract_unaudited +3 · il_risk +2 · lockup +2 · liquidation_risk +1 · centralized_dependency +1 · withdraw_queue +1 · depeg_risk +1
  • Tamaño (TVL): <$1M +2 · $1–10M +1 · $10–100M 0 · ≥$100M −1
  • Clase de activo: stable 0 · eth/btc/lp_token +1 · alt +2
  • Apalancamiento (leveraged_loop) +1 · dirección confianza media +1

Distribución: 13 Bajo ($1.76B) · 70 Medio ($377.3M) · 13 Alto ($21.4M). Bajo concentra el 82% del TVL (blue-chips de stablecoins).

Phase 4 — Report

Top 10 ranked by net_apy_30d − (risk_flag_count × 1%). CSV carries the 15 required columns plus underlying_asset_class.

Phase 5 — Validation

All 95 rows schema-complete (0 nulls in required columns); all addresses valid 0x/unique; every category, asset-class & risk-flag within its allowed set; APYs within 0–200%; TVL ≥ floor; every address re-verified on-chain for bytecode + symbol() match.

Exclusions (from 382 pools)

  • 205 raw AMM/DEX-LP & perp pools (not vaults)
  • 33 individual Morpho Blue markets — $2.65B, shared singleton, no distinct address (incl. $2.37B cbBTC)
  • 9 Extra Finance leverage-farms · 8 Compound collateral-only · 4 Pendle PT/LP
  • 4 duplicate DeFiLlama rows ($268M) · 4 sub-floor overcounts ($12M)
  • ~20 vaults with un-verifiable addresses
